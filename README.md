## Research Focus

### Portfolio Robustness Under Market Uncertainty (PRMU)

Independent research project analyzing how portfolios behave when traditional diversification assumptions break down.

Focus areas:
- Cross-asset and sector correlation compression  
- Volatility regime shifts and clustering  
- Failure modes of static allocation (Equal Weight, MVO)  
- Adaptive overlays using volatility-targeted exposure  

Initial findings indicate that regime-aware exposure adjustments can reduce drawdowns during periods of elevated correlation and volatility.

Presented at the 2026 Research and Creative Activities Symposium (RaCAS).

### TS-VSE (Trend Signals with Volatility-Scaled Exposure)

Systematic equity strategy designed to maintain controlled portfolio volatility while capturing directional trends.

Framework:
- Regime filter: price relative to 200-day moving average  
- Position sizing: volatility-targeted exposure using trailing realized volatility  
- Risk controls: predefined stop-loss and take-profit levels  
- Universe: liquid U.S. equities and ETFs  

Objective:
> Maintain consistent risk while allowing returns to compound through trend persistence.
